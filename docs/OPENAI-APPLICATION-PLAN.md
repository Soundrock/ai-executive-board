# AI Executive Board

## The AI Consensus Platform

### Tagline

Don't Ask One AI.
Ask An Entire AI Board.

---

## Problem

Today users are surrounded by many AI tools.

ChatGPT.
Gemini.
Claude.
DeepSeek.
Perplexity.
Copilot.

Each AI has strengths, weaknesses, biases, and blind spots.

Users often receive conflicting answers and do not know which recommendation to trust.

Most users also do not know which AI tool is best for a specific task.

---

## Solution

AI Executive Board transforms multiple AI systems into a collaborative AI decision board.

Instead of receiving a single AI opinion, users receive an AI consensus generated through multiple AI perspectives, validation, discussion, and reasoning.

---

## Innovation

From AI Answers To AI Consensus.

The project moves beyond single-model interactions and introduces a framework where multiple AI systems collaborate to generate higher-confidence recommendations.

---

## Why It Matters

The future of AI is not one model replacing every other model.

The future is multiple AI systems working together.

AI Executive Board makes this future accessible to everyday users, not only engineers.

---

## Open Source Mission

Build an open-source platform where anyone can access collective AI intelligence without needing to understand models, prompts, APIs, or agent frameworks.

---

## Long-Term Vision

Every person should have access to a personal AI executive team capable of helping them learn, decide, create, and solve problems.
